# AI Qadam — Brand Usage Policy

**Version:** v1 · 2026-06-19
**Contact:** brand@aiqadam.org

---

## What this covers

This policy governs the use of the AI Qadam brand identity — separately from
the MIT-licensed code in this repository (see `LICENSE`) and the CC BY 4.0
documentation content (see `LICENSE-content`).

The following are **brand assets** and not open source:

- The name **AI Qadam** and the wordmark **AI QADAM** (any casing).
- The footprint mark — the teal silhouette with four dots above it.
- The full lockup combining footprint + wordmark.
- The four-dot motif as a standalone graphic element.
- The brand teal (`#3CA29E` / `oklch(0.58 0.10 192)`) when used as a primary
  brand colour or logo background.
- The hashtag `#AIQadam`.

© 2026 AI Qadam Community. All rights reserved with respect to the assets
above.

---

## One rule above the rest

Partners contribute to the community; they do not acquire commercial rights
to the brand. Merch is funded as a contribution and distributed free.
Open-source projects can carry the brand only through community-lead
recognition. The brand is never a product partners can buy.

---

## Spirit

AI Qadam is a community held together by trust, not by structure. The
manifesto says it plainly: *partners invest in people and community — they
don't buy access to an audience.* This brand policy exists to keep that
promise enforceable. Anything that protects community trust is fine. Anything
that turns AI Qadam into an advertising channel for someone else is not.

When the letter and the spirit of this policy disagree, the spirit wins.

---

## Allowed without asking (Community use)

You may use AI Qadam brand assets for the following purposes, provided you
follow the visual rules in the guidelines:

- **Community chapters** authorised by the Global Board, in any country.
  Localise language and partners; keep the mark, wordmark, and brand teal
  unchanged.
- **Official AI Qadam events** — meetups, hackathons, Fuck-Up Nights, summits
  produced by or with an authorised chapter.
- **Participant materials** — speaker slides, personal social posts, articles,
  blog write-ups, retrospectives. Attribution welcome but not required.
- **Press and editorial coverage** of AI Qadam or its events, with accurate
  representation.
- **Educational use** in non-commercial talks, classes, and university
  curricula, with attribution.
- **Your own non-commercial merch** — a personal tote with the mark, a
  notebook for your study group, stickers for your meetup, a t-shirt for
  yourself. We don't restrict the format; we only ask that the identity
  stays intact (use the SVG from `/brand/`; don't redraw, recolour, or
  AI-generate variants). Paying a vendor for a one-off is fine — selling
  the result isn't (see *Not allowed* below).
- **Open-source projects recognised as part of AI Qadam Build.** Recognition
  is granted by community leads (the council of country leads + Global
  Board). Recognised projects may carry the AI Qadam Build mark and refer
  to themselves as "an AI Qadam Build project". Recognition can be revoked
  if the project diverges from community principles.

For all of the above: please use the SVG files from `/brand/` and follow the
clear-space and don't rules in `brand.html`.

## Allowed with a partnership agreement

The following require a written partnership agreement signed with the AI
Qadam Global Board or the relevant country lead:

- **Co-branding** on shared events, joint publications, or joint research.
- **Sponsorship recognition** on AI Qadam events, where the partner logo
  appears alongside the AI Qadam mark.
- **Funding production of community merch** — partners cover production cost
  as a contribution; merch is distributed free at AI Qadam events and never
  sold.

Partnership agreements are not contracts of audience access, and they are
not commercial licenses to the brand. They define mutual contribution to
the community. See the Partnership Deck for the canonical models (One-off ·
Monthly · Annual).

---

## Not allowed

The following uses are not permitted and will be challenged:

- **Commercial use of brand assets** in any product, service, or marketing.
  A partnership agreement is not a commercial license to the brand —
  partners contribute to the community, they do not acquire commercial
  rights to the marks, wordmark, or brand teal.
- **Selling merch carrying AI Qadam identity** — by anyone, including
  chapters, partners, third-party vendors, or individuals. Whether
  produced as a chapter give-away or as a personal one-off, items
  carrying the mark, wordmark, or brand teal are not for sale.
- **Self-declaring a project as "AI Qadam Build" or "an AI Qadam project"**
  without recognition from community leads.
- **Implying endorsement** by AI Qadam, the Global Board, any chapter, or
  any community member without that endorsement existing in writing.
- **Lead generation** disguised as an AI Qadam event, course, or community
  initiative.
- **Selling access to community members** — treating the AI Qadam audience
  as a sellable data set or marketing list.
- **Lookalike branding** — names, marks, hashtags, or wordmarks that could
  be confused with AI Qadam (`AI-Qadam`, `AI Kadam`, `Qadam AI`, etc.) used
  to attach to or compete with the community.
- **Derivative marks** — reworking the footprint, the four dots, or the
  wordmark for use as another organisation's logo.
- **Misrepresentation** — claiming chapter status, board membership, speaker
  status, or partnership where none has been granted.
- **Hateful, deceptive, or unlawful uses** of any AI Qadam asset.

---

## Enforcement

If you see a use that violates this policy, email
`brand@aiqadam.org` with a link and a short note. The Global Board
reviews complaints and responds in writing. We prefer a private conversation
first, a public correction next, and legal action only when neither has
worked.

If you're not sure whether your use is allowed, ask. Most cases get a
same-week yes.
