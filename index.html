About AI Qadam

A community of AI engineers, ML practitioners, and tech builders across Uzbekistan, Kazakhstan, and Tajikistan. We meet offline, share what's actually working, and avoid the conference fluff. Built by practitioners, for practitioners.

How to participate

Show up. Bring questions. Submit a talk — we run an open CFP for every meetup. Or co-host one in your city. We're community-owned, no corporate sponsorship strings, no "thought leader" panels.
